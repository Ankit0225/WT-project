Impact Ranking

KIIT Deemed to be University has been ranked in the cohort of 201-300 in the world by The Times Higher Education Impact Rankings 2021.

AICTE

KIIT University leads the rankings in first place – Ranked 1 for ’India Fights Corona’ award & the certificate of excellence for the same.

Eminence

The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Govt. of India has issued Letter of Intent (LoI) for ‘Institution of Eminence’ (IoE) tag to KIIT Deemed to be University, informed Dr. Achyuta Samanta, Founder, KIIT & KISS at a press meet in Bhubaneswar on September 7, 2019.

Young University

KIIT breaks into Times Higher Education Young University Rankings 2019. KIIT Deemed to be University, Bhubaneswar has been ranked 301+ in the 2019 Times Higher Education (THE) Young University Rankings.

The Awards Asia 2020

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) Deemed to be University has won the ‘THE Awards Asia’ 2020 declared by the Times Higher Education (THE) on 17th November 2020. KIIT, which has been declared winner in the category ‘Workplace of the Year’, is the only institute in India to get such prestigious recognition.

BRICS University Ranking

KIIT became the first private varsity in Odisha and eastern India to enter into the prestigious QS BRICS University Rankings for 2018 being ranked 251-300 in the ranking list.
